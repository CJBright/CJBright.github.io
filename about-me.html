A socially orientated person with a passion to learn new things!
My personal interests reflect these two qualities in almost every way, feeding into each other continuously.

Adventure.

Travel and exploration is my favourite pastime.
I love to become fully immersed into the culture to understand each region's uniquiness.
This has led me to learn Swedish at an intermediate level (previously fluent) and beginner Norwegian and Dutch.

Video-Games.

Surprisingly, video-games inspired my love for travel!
Visiting distant teammates and tournaments has taken me to Sweden, Norway, and Germany,
in addition to creating decade-long friendships from Slovenia, Russia, India, and more.
I particularly like team-strategy games (MOBAs) where communication and competition are paramount.

Tech.

Tech has always interested me and I love to learn about everything I can.
The thing I find most exciting about the technology field is that you can try your hand at everything, often for free!
From photo/video editing, digital art, 3D modelling, coding, server management, gaming - all on the same machine with no expensive tools.
I'd love to try my hand at an indie game for my next passion-project!
