I'm a socially orientated person with a passion to learn new things and a strong work ethic!
My personal interests reflect these qualities in every way, strengthening each other.

Adventure.

My favourite pastime is exploration and travel.
I most enjoy becoming fully immersed into the culture and language of each region to understand and embrace its uniquiness.
This has led me to learn Swedish at an intermediate level (previously fluent) and beginner Norwegian, Dutch, and Russian!

Video-Games.

Surprisingly, my experience with video-games inspired my love for travel.
Beginning as a way to visit distant teammates and progressing into attending tournaments, video games have taken me to Sweden, Norway, and Germany!
Additionally, I've formed decade-long friendships from Slovenia, India, Spain, and many more nations.
I particularly like team-strategy games (MOBAs) where communication and competition are paramount for success.

Tech.

Technology in all of its forms has interested me from a very early age.
I've spent countless hours learning as much as I can on computers, screen and sound technologies, cameras, printers, etc.
As somebody that likes to use their gadgets to breaking-point, I love the number of fields computer tech allows you to try, often for free:
• Photo editing • Video editing • Digital art • 3D modelling • Coding • Server management • Video-games
All performed on the same machine. For my next passion-project I'd love to try my hand at game development.
